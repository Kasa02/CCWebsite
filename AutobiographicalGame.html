Introduction:

This story is inspired by my own experiences as an international student. Before coming to the United States, I had heard many "stories" that often portray a negative image of this country. However, by living here and experiencing life firsthand, I realized that these preconceptions were just like the rumor in my story: exaggerated and one-sided.
By challenging and breaking down these biases, I found myself embracing a life that is richer, more hopeful, and full of opportunities. Just as the mouse discovered that the cat was not the terrifying predator he feared, I learned that the reality of a place can often be far more nuanced and positive than the tales we hear from afar.
